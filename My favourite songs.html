MY FAVOURITE SONGS

1.Maneke Magehite

This song is sung by Yohani and Satheeshan . Written by Yohani , Satheeshan , Chamath Sangeeth. This song was originally sung in sinhala language and was dubbed in in many languages like telugu , tamil , hindi , punjabi , nepali etc. It was released on july 2020[original music videa] and 22 May 2021 [cover music video by yohani]. The Tamil and Malayalam versions of the song were sung by Yohani were published on youtube on 28 August 2021. The song surprassed 100 million views in Youtube as of september 2021 .

2. Ramulo Ramula

This song is in the movie Ala Vaikuntapuramulo. This song is sung by Anurag Kulkarni and Mangli and was composed by SS Thaman.

3. Idhe kadha nee katha

This song is in the movie Maharshi. This song is sung by Vijay Prakash. There is a famous dialogue in this movie that is "SUCCESS IS NOT A DESTINATION , SUCCESS IS JOURNEY".
